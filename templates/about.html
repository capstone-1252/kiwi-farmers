About

Who Are We?

Hello! My name is Ruby Su, the owner and sole masseuse at Top Asian Massage and Foot Reflexology. I have been practicing massage therapy since 2019 after graduating from the massage therapy program at MaKami College in Edmonton Alberta. After graduating, I worked at a Chinese massage clinic for 3 years, where I developed strong skills in therapeutic massage. I was confident enough to start my own practice out of my own studio in 2022. What was originally meant to be a side hustle to supplement my income quickly became my full-time career.

For a long time, I've always had a passion for pain management and traditional Chinese techniques, so wanted to find a career path that would allow me to help others in their healing journeys. Before I found massage therapy, I intended to be a nurse, but unfortunately this wasn't the career for me, as I found myself very uncomfortable using needles. Not wanting my fear of needles to result in any unfortunate circumstances, I decided to shift my focus to a gentler form of care. From there, I enrolled into a 2-year massage therapy course in 2017, and the rest is history.

Our History

Working for myself has been extremely rewarding. Having the flexibility to work my own hours and providing personalized, high-quality care for my clients has given me a strong sense of independence and passion for my craft. I am eternally grateful for all the success my independent practice has brought, no doubt by the support I've received from all my customers from opening day up to today. It fills me with a sense of pride to be able to help members of my local community feel better in their own body, whether that be through a simple relaxation massage or helping alleviate chronic pain, and I hope to share this passion with you should you choose to book a service with me.
